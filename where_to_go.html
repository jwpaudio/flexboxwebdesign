Where Can I Go For My First Dive?

Types of Diving Locations

There are many different types of places you can go on your first dive. It is a common misconception that you need to be near an ocean in order to get certified. The following is a list of possible first diving locations:

  • The Ocean
  • Lakes
  • Resovoirs
  • Rivers
  • Streams
  • Caves

Things to Look For

When looking for a dive shop to sign up for your first dive there are two important things that you will want to look for. The first is that the dive shop is well reviewed. This can be done by searching the dive shop on Google and reading the Google reviews. Make sure there are plenty of satisfied divers in the review section. The second thing to look for is that they are certfied by one of the two major diving certifying bodies. This would be either through PADI (Professional Association of Diving Instructors) or through SSI (Scuba Schools International).

Scheduling Your First Dive

To schedule your first dive, call up the dive shop and see what availability they have. Most dive shops give you the option to either start with an exploratory dive or go right into a certification class. Make sure to ask about pricing during this initial conversation so you know what to expect.

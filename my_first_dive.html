What Can I Expect on My First Dive?

  1. Day One

    2. On your first day you will meet with your dive instructor and go over the schedule for the day. Your first day will be spent at the pool. You can expect to spend a few hours in the pool with the instructor learning essential diving skills such as how to communicate under water and how to use your diviving equipment.

  2. Day Two

    3. After you've completed your pool training, you are ready to go on an open water dive with your instructor. They will walk you through their expectations and lay out what your first dive will look like. Most open water dives start early in the morning and can last most of the day depending on how long the boat ride is to the dive site.

  3. Following Days

    4. If you only booked an exploratory dive, you may already be finished diving. If you are pursuing a diving certification, it is likely that you will spend an additional day doing more open water diving.

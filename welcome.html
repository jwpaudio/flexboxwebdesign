Welcome!

Purpose

If you're reading this then that means you are interested in joining the exciting sport of scuba diving. The purpose of this site is to give a brief overview of the information and concepts that prospective divers need to have a great first dive.

Navigation

Using the menu bar at the top, you can navigate through the site to find information about where to go for your first dive, what to expect on your first dive, information about certification and diving equipment as well as a page with additional resources to help you get started. After your first dive is over, come back to this page and leave a review to help other beginner divers realize how much fun they are missing out on!

What Equipment Will I Use When I Dive?

In order to go diving safely, each diver must have the appropriate diving equipment. On your first dive it is likely you will rent most of the equipment but it is still important to know each of the items you will need when you dive. Below is a list of the most common equipment and a description of each item.

Scuba Mask
Creates air space in front of your eyes and allows them to focus underwater.
Scuba Fins
Fins translate power from the large leg muscles into efficient movement through water.
Wetsuit
Exposure suits insulate you against the cooling effect of water.
Scuba BCDs
Holds your gear in place, lets you carry a tank with minimal effort, floats you at the surface and allows you to achieve neutral bouyancy at any depth.
Scuba Regulators
Converts high-pressure air in your tank to ambient pressure so you can breathe it. It also delivers are to other places, such as your BCD.
Dive Computers
By constantly monitoring depth and bottom time, dive computers automatically calculate your no-decompression status. They can also monitor your ascent rate and your tank pressure.
